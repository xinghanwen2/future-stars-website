Youth Baseball Development

Helping young baseball players improve through video, data, and clear feedback.

Future Stars Baseball Club is a youth baseball development project focused on structured hitting analysis, player reports, training goals, and before/after progress tracking.

Register for a Session View Case Studies

What We Do

Step 1

Video Capture

Players are recorded from consistent angles so their swing can be reviewed clearly and compared over time.

Step 2

Player Report

Each report focuses on a few clear strengths, improvement areas, and simple next steps.

Step 3

Retest & Compare

Players can retest after several weeks to compare progress and update the training focus.

How the Analysis Works

  1. Parent completes registration and consent.
  2. Player profile is created.
  3. Video clips and simple metrics are collected.
  4. Vincent completes baseball analysis and main findings.
  5. A short player report is delivered to the family.
  6. A retest is scheduled to compare before and after progress.
