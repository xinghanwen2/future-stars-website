Anonymous Player Case Studies

Privacy rule: Use anonymous labels such as “Player A, Age 12.” Public case studies require parent approval.
Sample

Player A, Age 12

Initial issue: Inconsistent contact during front toss.

Video finding: Contact point was often too far forward, causing weak ground balls.

Training focus: Balance, timing, and staying through the ball.

Retest goal: Improve contact consistency after 2-4 weeks.

Sample

Player B, Age 10

Initial issue: Swing path was too long.

Video finding: Hands moved away from the body before entering the hitting zone.

Training focus: Shorter hand path and better finish balance.

Retest goal: More repeatable barrel contact.
