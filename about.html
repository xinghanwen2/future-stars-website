About the Club

Why Future Stars Baseball Club Exists

Future Stars Baseball Club was created to help young players see their swing clearly, understand their main issues, receive practical drills, and track progress over time.

From youth coaching to a structured program

Future Stars Baseball Club was formally registered in July 2025, but the work behind the club began earlier. Vincent Hall started helping younger baseball players during his freshman year of high school, working with players ages 6–12 on basic hitting and throwing skills.

Through those early coaching experiences, he noticed that many young players struggled with recurring problems such as inconsistent balance, late timing, early front-shoulder opening, long swing paths, unstable contact points, and difficulty turning general advice into actual movement changes.

As Vincent continued developing as a player himself, he began combining baseball experience with coaching insight, structured video review, simple data tracking, and individualized feedback.
