Method

Video Analysis, Data Tracking, and Retest Method

This is the most important professional page on the website. It shows that Future Stars is a structured player development system, not just general youth coaching.

Core Method

1. Video Analysis

Two standard camera angles are used to record each player’s swing during tee work and front toss.

2. Data Tracking

Track contact quality, direction, timing, balance, bat path, and contact point.

3. Training Drills

Each player receives 2–3 clear, targeted drills based on his swing needs.

4. Retest

Follow-up sessions compare baseline vs retest to show progress over time.

Standard Setup

  • Side View
  • Back / Catcher View
  • Recommended recording quality: 1080p / 60fps
  • Test types: Tee Hitting and Front Toss

What We Review

  • Balance
  • Timing
  • Bat Path
  • Contact Point
  • Overall repeatability
