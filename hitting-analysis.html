Hitting Analysis Process

This page explains the repeatable process used for each player: registration, video capture, analysis, report, training plan, and retest.

Required Video Angles

Side View

Used to review stance, load, stride, hip rotation, and contact position.

Back View

Used to review bat path, swing plane, plate coverage, and contact direction.

Front / Oblique View

Used to review hand path, shoulder direction, balance, and overall movement.

Analysis Scorecard

Category What We Review Score
Stance & Balance Body control before and after the swing 1-5
Load & Timing Rhythm, start time, and ability to adjust 1-5
Stride Direction Whether the stride supports strong contact 1-5
Hip Rotation How the lower body contributes to power 1-5
Bat Path Efficiency of the swing path through the hitting zone 1-5
Contact Point Where contact happens and how consistent it is 1-5

Report Process

  1. Collect player profile, goals, and parent consent.
  2. Record consistent swing clips from the required angles.
  3. Store video links and metrics in the backend spreadsheet.
  4. Vincent identifies 1-3 main improvement areas.
  5. Create a 3-5 page PDF player report.
  6. Schedule a 2-4 week retest to compare progress.
