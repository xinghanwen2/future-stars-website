Founder

Vincent Hall — Founder & Hitting Analysis Lead

Vincent Hall is a high school student-athlete and baseball player at Maranatha High School in Pasadena, California. He plays infield and has competed in both school and club baseball while also coaching younger players in the community.

Founder Overview

Vincent’s interest in youth coaching began during his freshman year of high school, when he started helping players ages 6–12 develop basic hitting and throwing skills. He saw that many young players were working hard but did not always understand what was happening in their own swing.

That observation became the foundation for Future Stars Baseball Club. Vincent created the club to combine baseball experience, structured video review, simple data tracking, and individualized feedback in a way that helps young players understand what they are doing well, what needs to improve, and which drills can help them move forward.
